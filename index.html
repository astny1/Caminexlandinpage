About CAMINEX

Zambia’s premier trade exhibition for the mining, industrial, agricultural, and engineering sectors.

CAMINEX (Copperbelt Agricultural, Mining, Industrial, Networking, Enterprise) is Zambia’s premier trade exhibition held annually in Kitwe — the heart of the Copperbelt. It serves as a key business and networking platform for companies in the mining, industrial, agricultural, and engineering sectors.

The exhibition brings together local and international industry leaders, investors, government representatives, and professionals to showcase the latest technologies, products, and services driving Zambia’s and the region’s economic growth. CAMINEX provides a unique space for knowledge exchange, innovation display, and business partnerships across multiple industries.

CAMINEX is organised by the Copperbelt Agricultural and Commercial Show Society and is held annually at the Showgrounds in Kitwe. The event continues to grow each year, attracting both local and international participants who come together to share ideas, explore opportunities, and foster business growth.
